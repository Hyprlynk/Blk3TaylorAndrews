Our Mission

Taylor Andrews' mission combines the protection of american values with new, unique programs to grow the economy. In the place of ineffective programs like welfare, Taylor Andrews has proposed a new system in which faith-based organizations compete for federal funds to provide social services. His 3-year guest work program will easily provide jobs for (legal) immigrants looking to get a leg up in their new home. Andrews will also roll back restrictions on gun ownership, so happy hunting, fellow gun-lovers!

Stances At A Glance

Taxes: The government should enact tax cuts at all level, especially
on the rich.

Crime: Minimum sentences should be required for certain crimes.
The death penalty should be legal in every state.

Environment: Environmental restrictions should be rolled back and
business should be unregulated.

Foreign Policy: The US military should take action immediately
whenever the interests of the country are threatened.

Healthcare: Healthcare should be a private business with no additional
federal funding.

Social Services: The government should stop funding social welfare
programs. The poor should work harder, because anyone can
become successful in america with hard work and dedication.

Immigration: The government should crack down on illegal
immigration and seek out and deport illegal aliens.
